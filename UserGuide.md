## South Sudan Scenario
### Background on South Sudan

The Republic of South Sudan gained  its independence from the Republic of the Sudan in 2011. Soon after its inception, political instability followed due to a power struggle. The country has been immersed in civil conflict since December 2013, resulting in severely damaged agriculture, disrupted food production and distribution, loss of life, health, assets and income. South Sudan has some of the worst health indicators in the world<sup>[1](http://www.sudantribune.com/spip.php?article1616), [2](https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC1179754)</sup>.
